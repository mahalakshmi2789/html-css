Definitions

Shiva
He is the Supreme Being in Shaivism, one of the major traditions within Hinduism. Shiva is known as The Destroyer within the Trimurti,
the Hindu trinity which also includes Brahma and Vishnu. In the Shaivite tradition,
Shiva is the Supreme Lord who creates, protects and transforms the universe.
Muruga
Murugan, chief deity of the ancient Tamils of South India,
son of the warrior goddess Korravai. He was later identified in part with the North Indian war god Skanda.
His favourite weapon was the trident or spear, and his banner carried the emblem of a wild fowl
